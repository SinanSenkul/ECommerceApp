# Privacy Policy

**Smart E-Commerce**  
**Effective Date: April 13, 2026**

Smart E-Commerce (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By using Smart E-Commerce, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

- **Account Information**: Email address and password when you create an account or place an order.

We do not collect sensitive information such as location (unless you voluntarily provide a delivery address), biometric data, or health data.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve our super-simple shopping experience
- Process your orders and deliver products
- Send order confirmations and important updates
- Prevent fraud and ensure security

## 3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only in these cases:
- With trusted service providers who help us run the app (hosting, analytics, payment processing, delivery partners) — they are contractually required to protect your data.
- When required by law (e.g., court orders).
- In the event of a business transfer (merger, acquisition, etc.).

## 4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no system is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR & KVKK)

You have the right to:
- Access, correct, or delete your personal data
- Object to or restrict processing
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Receive your data in a portable format
